My Cooking

I like experimenting with recipes that catch my interest, since a lot of the food I take interest in is either really hard to find or outright unavailable where I live... or it's just expensive.

Schweinshaxe

German roasted pork knuckles. Commonly sold at the Night Market, but it got pricy, so I started making my own.



Chicken Cream Stew

A recipe I learned from a game. It's a bit thicker than usual cream stews.



Griddle Fried Salmon

I recently got a new griddle at the time I made this, so I decided to use it to cook up an entire salmon.



Chicken and Sausage Gumbo

Before my family stopped buying Campbell soup cans, I came to like the flavour of chicken and sausage gumbo. I recently started missing it, so I started making my own. The first time ended up being so spicy, no one could eat it until I added cheese to it.
