Welcome to my web page!

Name's Kyo~ I'm an artist who specializes in drawing with a manga-inspired art style and designing very detailed mechanical weaponry for fun! (Provided I'm not distracted with games.)

As of right now, I'm studying IT while learning how to make games and forming the occasional team to make them. Aside from that, I also also cook for a hobby.

If any of this interests you, I have a gallery that you can access from the navigation bar on the left or further down below!

My Cooking

I like experimenting with recipes that catch my interest, since a lot of the food I take interest in is either really hard to find or outright unavailable where I live... or it's just expensive.

Schweinshaxe

German roasted pork knuckles. Commonly sold at the Night Market, but it got pricy, so I started making my own.



Chicken Cream Stew

A recipe I learned from a game. It's a bit thicker than usual cream stews.



Griddle Fried Salmon

I recently got a new griddle at the time I made this, so I decided to use it to cook up an entire salmon.



Chicken and Sausage Gumbo

Before my family stopped buying Campbell soup cans, I came to like the flavour of chicken and sausage gumbo. I recently started missing it, so I started making my own. The first time ended up being so spicy, no one could eat it until I added cheese to it.



Games I've Worked On

Over my time at KPU, I've worked on several games and collaborated with artists I've met online to bring a few of my ideas to life in game format.

Magic 8-Burrito

My first collaboration project with rabu-nee. This is a triple purpose "magic 8 ball" app written in Processing 4 with help from rabu, who helped me figure out the bugs and also provided the character art. My own art contributions comprised of just the clouds and the button art due to the limited time I had to complete this game.

Fading Steel: Tactical Firearms Operations

An in-development point and clock shooting game by Team Crankshaft where I am serving as the main artist and graphic designer. Development is currently on hold due to the main programmer being unavailable at the time of writing. Mockups can be found on the gallery page!

Knight's Quest: Hunter and Rune

A JRPG that ended up being a failed project. The original version of Hunter's Quest was written in Processing 4, but countless problems during development caused the project to fall apart. KQ:H&R is currently a planned project with rabu-nee to make an expanded remake using a JRPG game engine; introducing new ideas and more fleshed out animations.
